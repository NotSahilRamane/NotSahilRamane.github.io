About Me

I'm a 23 year old Electronics Engineer from Mumbai. I work mostly on projects in Robotics, Machine Learning and Automotive space, and since the start of this year, I've been working in the electric mobility domain in a company called Energy In Motion.

I'm fortunate to have worked on several ambitious projects since the beginning of my engineering journey. It kicked off with Orion Racing India, Team KJSCE Robocon, and other megaprojects at my university — KJSCE, Mumbai (then affiliated with Mumbai University). I’ve briefly worked at DORLECO on ADAS development, and recently at Praan solving pollution using Computer Vision and Robotics.

In the past, I’ve had the opportunity to work on autonomous and electric mobility systems, drones, underwater robots, low-cost mapping systems, assistive technologies for the differently-abled, climate tech solutions, and other robotic systems solving niche problems.

I usually post about my work and experiments on my Instagram.

Projects has some snippets of various projects I’ve worked on — not necessarily in chronological order.
